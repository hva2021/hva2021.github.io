About Cookie Clicker


A browser-based idle game called Cookie Clicker was created in 2013 by French programmer Julien "Orteil" Thiennot. The object of the game is to click on a big cookie on the screen to collect "cookies," which can then be used to buy structures that generate additional cookies on their own. As the player advances, they may unlock additional structures and gameplay features like upgrades and new locations, which enable them to make cookies even more quickly. The game has no end, thus the player may keep playing eternally while attempting to produce cookies at the best potential pace.

The idea of "idle" gaming—in which the game advances even when the user isn't actively playing—is one of Cookie Clicker's core gameplay components. This enables them to leave the game running and return at a later time to collect their earned cookies and make additional building purchases.
